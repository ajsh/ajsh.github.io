ABOUT

Hey, I'm Aayush. I drink tea and code all day, everyday! Constantly involved in building projects, that are either for my fun-time, course-work projects or for client's business.

I hold an Associates Degree in Computer Engineering from Gujarat Technological University and currently pursuing Bachelor's in Computer Science at Iowa State University. At ISU, I serve as a Web Master for Computer Science and Software Engineering Club, where I maintain Club's websites and social media, along with helping the committee in organizing Webinar, Company event, and Hackathon.

I work at ResearchIT (ISU), where I develop software and web applications to aid difficult computational issues faced during research work for Professors and Ph.D. candidates. Previously, I worked as a Teaching Assistant for Com S 113 (Prof. Sui-Hui Chang), Child Welfare and Human Research (Moodle Plugin Developer), Civil and Env. Engg. (Full Stack), Upward Bound TRIO (Taught Hindi), and few more.

I am an incoming Intern at Wellmark for Summer 2018, as a JavaScript/MEAN Stack Developer. I am excited about this opportunity as the Health Care Industry has always fascinated me with the idea that even a small impact can lead to a healthier tomorrow for millions.

I believe in continuous learning and research, leading to seek new technologies and methods for creating rich and easy to use software products.
