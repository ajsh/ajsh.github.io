Epi Predictor, is a one of my personal & touching project. Epilepsy is a neurologic disorder causing uncontrollable seizure episodes. Over 65 million people live with the condition. Timely and accurate seizure prediction can help patients to take control of their lives.

EpiPredict is to integrate EEG handset technology (provided by Muse) to clasify and modal EEG data that signals a seizure and alarm the loved ones before the seizure takes palce. During our course research we found accurate EEG modals can predict seizures hours in advance.

Roles: Android / Backend / Deep Neural Network Model.
Technologies: rest-api, influxdb, tensorflow, keras, machine-learning, muse-headband, android-studio, java, python, json, iot, webapi.
